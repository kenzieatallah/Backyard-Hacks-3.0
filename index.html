shop 4 sustainability

Here at S.4.S our purpose is to reduce emmisions to further the development of sustainability. Now, more than ever, it's imperative that we purposefully protect our quality of life and Earth’s ecosystems.

Ik, sounds boringgg. But it doesn't have to be! At S.4.S you can live out your retail therapy dreams in good conscience. Shop 4 sustainability by purchasing popular makeup products from strictly your fav eco-friendly makeup brands. And if you thought it couldn't get any better, wait till you hear this: one tree is planted for every $50 you spend on the products listed on our site using promo code S4S!

For those of you who fancy getting your hands dirty and love free things as much as we do, click the Volunteer button and fill out the form to help us with our tree-planting endeavours. We ♡ our volunteers, so much so that we send them a monthly makeup bundle free of charge!
